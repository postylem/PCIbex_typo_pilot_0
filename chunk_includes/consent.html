Information for Participants

Thank you for your interest in our study. Please carefully read the following information and then decide whether or not you would like to participate in this study.

  • Scientific Aim: In this research study, we investigate how people understand and process language in real time during reading, using the self-paced reading paradigm.
  • Study Procedure: We will ask you some basic demographic questions before starting. During the study, we will present you with sentences which you can read at your own pace, and then ask you to answer questions by clicking on an appropriate answer. The entire experiment should take around 12 minutes to complete. Detailed instructions will be provided if you consent to participate.
  • Benefits:
    • For participants: You get an insight into our research. If interested, we are happy to explain the exact intentions of this experiment afterwards.
    • For others and Science: Insights into language processing.
  • Risk: There are no known risks or side effects.
  • Obligations of the Participants: The success of this study depends on your cooperation. We ask you to work concentrated and in accordance with the instructions during the entire time.
  • Confidentiality and Handling of Data:The recorded data from the study will be stored anonymously and treated strictly confidentially. There is no possibility for the public to trace your data at any time.
  • Compensation: You will be provided with a link back to Prolific after completion of the experiment, clicking on this link will confirm your participation, and direct Prolific to provide your compensation.
  • Voluntariness of Participation: Your participation in the study is voluntary. You will not be disadvantaged by not participating in the study.
  • Possibility of Withdrawal from the Study: You may terminate your voluntary participation in the study at any time and without giving reasons, without any disadvantages resulting from it. The study leader may also decide to terminate the entire study or your participation early if this is necessary.
  • Responsible Contact Person during the Study: If you have questions or problems, please contact the study leader: Jacob Louis Hoover (jacob.hoover [at] mail .mcgill .ca).
