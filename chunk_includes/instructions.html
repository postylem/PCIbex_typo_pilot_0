Instructions

Your task in this experiment is to read sentences. These will be shown to you word by word. First, an asterisk (*) will appear.

*

Press Space when you are ready to start the first sentence. First you will only see dashes indicating the length of the sentence:

___ ___ ______ ___ ____

To see the first word of the sentence, please press Space:

The ___ ______ ___ ____

As soon as you press Space again, the next word will appear and the previous word will disappear. Keep pressing Space to proceed through the sentence.

___ dog ______ ___ ____
___ ___ chased ___ ____
___ ___ ______ teh ____
___ ___ ______ ___ cat.

Read the sentences at your own normal reading pace, so that you understand the content. Note, some of the sentences may have words which are incorrectly spelled.

Press Space after you have read a sentence to see the next one.

Sentences are followed by a comprehension question with two answer options. Please answer to this question by clicking on the most appropriate response.

Once you have given an answer, you cannot revise it. Just continue with the next sentence. A comprehension question might look like this:

What animal chased the cat?
1. A cat
2. A dog

First, you will practice the task using 4 examples. Then the actual experiment will follow.
